Welcome to the Apples Website!

On this website, you can learn about different types of apples and their characteristics.

Red Delicious

Red Delicious apples are known for their sweet taste and bright red skin. They are a popular choice for snacking and salads.

Granny Smith

Granny Smith apples are tart and crisp, with a bright green skin. They are a popular choice for baking and cooking.

Honeycrip

Honeycrip apples are sweet and juicy, with a yellow-green skin. They are a popular choice for snacking and apple sauce.
